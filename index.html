About Us

Syndicated Loan Direct provides analysis and comps on syndicated loan deals for professionals in banks, law firms, asset management and Private Equity firms. Our analysis is granular and shows you underlying risks and current market trends.

Syndicated Loan Direct provides a solution in which subscribing participants have access to a fast and flexible platform with high quality deal data via an intuitive search interface.
